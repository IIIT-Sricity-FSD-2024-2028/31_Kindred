How It Works

1
Select a role card above — credentials are auto-filled on the Sign In page.
2
Click "Sign In" — you'll be routed to the correct dashboard based on your role.
3
Super User: Add/Edit/Delete organizations, approve/reject requests, view analytics.
Admin: Add/Edit volunteers & programs, accept/decline incoming requests.
End Users: View dashboard, submit requests, browse campaigns.
4
All data is in-memory — changes persist within the browser session. Refresh resets to initial data.
