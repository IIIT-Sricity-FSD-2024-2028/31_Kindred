# TeamName : 31_Kindred
# Domain : Non-Profit & NGO Operations
# Problem Statement: __Volunteer and Resource Coordination System__


Non-profit organizations and NGOs face significant challenges in coordinating volunteers, managing donations, supporting beneficiaries, and tracking resources effectively. The lack of a centralized system leads to:
* Inefficient volunteer management: Difficulty in matching volunteers with appropriate opportunities, tracking their contributions, and maintaining engagement
* Limited donation transparency: Donors lack visibility into how their contributions are being used and the impact they're creating
* Fragmented beneficiary support: Inconsistent processes for beneficiary registration, assistance delivery, and progress tracking
* Resource allocation challenges: Poor visibility into resource inventory, utilization, and distribution across programs

This system aims to provide a comprehensive platform that streamlines operations, enhances transparency, improves engagement, and maximizes the impact of non-profit organizations.

# Identified Actors
The system supports four primary user roles:
* Volunteer: An individual who willingly contributes time and skills to support NGO activities without receiving financial compensation.

* Donor: A person or organization that provides financial or material support to the NGO to fund programs.

* Beneficiary - Individuals or groups who receive assistance and support from the organization

* Admin - System administrators and organizational staff who manage operations, users, and programs

# Actor Features:

## __Volunteer Features__

__1. Search and apply for Opportunities__

* View opportunity details: Browse comprehensive information about available volunteer positions
* Advanced search with filters: Find opportunities based on location, skills, time commitment, and cause area
* Submit applications with a custom message and select preferred dates or shifts: Apply for positions with personalized messages and scheduling preferences
* Track application status: Monitor the progress of applications from submission to acceptance
* Withdraw applications and provide a reason for withdrawal: Cancel applications with the ability to explain the reason

__2. Communicate & Network__

* Direct messaging with program coordinators and supervisors: Communicate directly with organizational leaders
* Connect with fellow volunteers and build a professional network: Build relationships with other volunteers
* Share field updates, photos, and stories from volunteer activities: Post updates and media from volunteer work
* Receive announcements and organizational updates: Stay informed about news and organizational information

__3. Request Resources & Support__

* Resource Request Submission: Submit requests for materials or support needed for volunteer activities
* Knowledge Base and FAQ Access: Access self-service resources and answers to common questions
* Priority and Urgency Classification: Categorize requests based on importance and time sensitivity
* Feedback and Request Closure: Provide feedback on support received and close resolved requests

## Donor Features

__1. Make a donation__

* Choose between one-time and recurring donation options: Select flexible donation frequencies
* Campaign and Project Selection: Direct contributions to specific campaigns or projects
* Process secure payments through multiple methods: Complete transactions using various secure payment options
* Donation Confirmation and Tracking: Receive instant confirmation and tracking capabilities
* display Tax Receipt: Access digital tax receipts for record-keeping

__2. Track Donation Impact__

* View a personalized impact dashboard showing how funds were used: See visual representations of donation utilization
* Receive periodic updates on funded programs: Get regular progress reports on supported initiatives
* Access photos and stories from beneficiaries: View authentic stories and images from those helped
* Donation history and cumulative impact: Review complete donation history and total impact
* Download annual giving statements: Access comprehensive annual statements for tax purposes

## Beneficiary Features

__1. Register & Apply for Assistance__

* Complete registration with household information: Provide comprehensive household details during registration
* Provide demographic information for program eligibility assessment: Submit information to determine program eligibility
* Manage privacy settings and provide consent for data sharing: Control privacy preferences and data sharing permissions
* Update emergency contact information and medical requirements: Maintain current emergency contacts and health information
* Specify accessibility needs: Indicate any accessibility requirements for better service delivery

__2. Beneficiary Support & Engagement__

* Send secure messages to assigned case managers and coordinators: Communicate securely with support staff
* Submit detailed feedback through structured surveys and open comments: Provide comprehensive feedback through multiple formats
* Report issues or complaints with priority levels and attach supporting evidence: Document and escalate concerns with relevant documentation
* Schedule virtual or in-person meetings with case workers: Arrange consultations with support personnel
* Share success stories and testimonials anonymously or publicly: Contribute testimonials to showcase program impact

## Admin Features

__1. Manage Users__

* Create, edit, and deactivate user accounts for all actor types: Complete user account lifecycle management
* Assign roles and permissions to users: Configure access levels and capabilities
* Reset passwords and manage security settings: Handle security administration tasks
* Generate user activity reports and audit trails: Monitor system usage and maintain compliance records

__2. Volunteer Management__

* Assign volunteers and allocate resources to programs: Match volunteers with appropriate opportunities
* Track volunteer hours: Monitor volunteer time contributions
* Assign volunteers and allocate resources to programs: Distribute volunteers and resources across initiatives
* Verify volunteer credentials and conduct background checks: Ensure volunteer qualifications and safety
* Set travel radius and transportation preferences for volunteers: Configure volunteer mobility parameters

__3. Program and Performance Management__

* Create new programs with defined objectives, timelines, and resource requirements: Establish new initiatives with complete specifications
* Set milestones and track program progress: Monitor achievement of program goals
* Archive completed programs and generate impact reports: Preserve historical data and measure outcomes
* Generate analytical report: Create comprehensive reports on program performance and outcomes

__4. Resource management__

* Volunteer Activity Tracking: Monitor volunteer participation and engagement levels
* Resource Utilization Reports: Track how resources are being used across programs
* Maintenance and Expiry Tracking: Monitor resource maintenance schedules and expiration dates
* Resource Allocation and Distribution: Manage resource allocation and distribution across programs
